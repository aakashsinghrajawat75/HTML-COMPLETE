The Preformatted Tag

So, how do you fix the whitespace problem if you actually want to display spaces and line breaks exactly as you typed them?

Jay suggests using the <pre> tag!

Regular Paragraph:

My Bonnie lies over the ocean. My Bonnie lies over the sea. My Bonnie lies over the ocean. Oh, bring back my Bonnie to me.

Preformatted Text:

        My Bonnie lies over the ocean.
        My Bonnie lies over the sea.
        My Bonnie lies over the ocean.
        Oh, bring back my Bonnie to me.

Notice how the pre tag preserves the enters, the tabs, and usually displays text in a fixed-width (courier) font.
